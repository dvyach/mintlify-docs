    ### 1. Welcome Page
    As you open the Nanoheal client, Welcome page will be  displayed  as  shown  in  the  below figure. There are multiple options on this page. You can click on the 'Click to Troubleshoot', button on the page to access the trouble-shooters page. You can also click on the burger menu on top to explore other functions. To come back to welcome page, at any time, click on home button on the top-left of the client to come back to this page. There are two images below
        1.	Welcome page with different options that can be explored by clicking on burger icon
        2.	Troubleshooter page showing home button on top-left to navigate back to welcome page.

    ### 2. Troubleshooter’s Page
    Troubleshooters page list all the automations that are available for end-user to execute. There are 2 ways to navigate to Troubleshooter’s page:
      1.	On the Welcome page, click on the “Click to Troubleshoot”, button to access the Troubleshooters page.
      2.	From anywhere in the Nanoheal client, you can click on top right burger icon and click on the Troubleshooters option to open this page.

Please refer to the 2 images below to see the troubleshooters page and the second  way  to navigate to the troubleshooter’s page.

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="https://nanoheal-enterprise.s3.amazonaws.com/Troubleshooter.jpg"
/>

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}

/>
On the left hand, it displays all the categories of automations  that  are  available  for  end  user  to execute. User can navigate to relevant category and look for the  required  automations  / troubleshooters. Click on one of the items from the list on the left to see the corresponding executable automations and tile descriptions. The automation / solution will be accompanied by “Execute” button. If the execute button is not displayed, then that would mean, it is sub-category and can further be expanded. Below 2 images show both type of pages:
    1.	By clicking on a category on left hand panel, you see the automations / troubleshooters on the right side
    2.	By clicking on a category on left hand panel, you see sub-categories on the right side. By clicking on the plus button in the sub-category, the automation list is opened up


    ### Option 3: System Tray
    Nanoheal client can also be accessed using start-menu. User can click on start and search for Nanoheal. Click on the Nanoheal client to open it.
